Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create intelligent machines. 
It enables systems to learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows computers to learn without being explicitly programmed. 
Deep Learning is a further subset that uses neural networks with many layers.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) helps machines understand and generate human language.

AI is widely used in applications such as chatbots, recommendation systems, fraud detection, and autonomous vehicles.
